Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

What's your development process?

I follow an agile approach: Discovery & Planning → Design & Prototyping → Development → Testing → Launch → Maintenance. You'll be involved throughout the process with regular updates and feedback sessions.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! I offer various maintenance packages including bug fixes, security updates, content updates, and feature enhancements. I'm here to ensure your website continues to perform optimally.

What technologies do you work with?

I specialize in modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and various cloud platforms. I choose the best tech stack for each project.

Can you work with my existing team?

I'm experienced in collaborating with designers, project managers, and other developers. I can integrate seamlessly into your existing workflow and communication tools.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

I include a revision period with every project, and I'm always available for additional changes or enhancements. I'll provide clear pricing for any additional work beyond the original scope.
